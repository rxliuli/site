# Twitter Filter

## Introduction

Filter your Twitter/X timeline using customizable rules. Mark, hide, or auto-block tweets based on account properties and tweet content.

Tired of spam, crypto promotion, or low-quality tweets cluttering your feed? Twitter Filter lets you build your own filtering rules with a simple expression language — or subscribe to community-published rules.

## Key Features

- 🎯 Expression-based rules with access to user and tweet fields
- 👀 Three actions: mark, hide, or auto-block matched tweets
- 📡 Subscribe to community rules — updates sync automatically
- 📋 Screen name lists up to 100,000 entries per rule, importable from CSV
- ✅ Whitelist your own tweets and accounts you follow
- 🔒 Filtering runs entirely in your browser — no timeline data leaves your device

Works on all major browsers — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari — including mobile (Kiwi, Edge Canary on Android, Safari on iOS).
