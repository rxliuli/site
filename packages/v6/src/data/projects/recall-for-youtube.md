# Recall for YouTube

A browser extension that enables full-text search across your YouTube watch history.

## Features

- **Auto Import History**: Automatically imports your YouTube watch history on first use
- **Full-Text Search**: Search across video titles, channel names, and descriptions
- **Local Storage**: All data stored locally in your browser - no cloud sync, privacy first
- **Quick Access**: Press `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+K` (macOS) to open search
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Automatically records new videos as you watch

## Usage

1. Install the extension and visit YouTube
2. Your watch history will be automatically imported on first use
3. Press `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+K` (macOS) to open the search panel
4. Type to search through your watch history
5. Click on a result to navigate to the video
