# Privacy Policy

> Last updated: 2026-05-12

## Introduction

Welcome to our browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

## Data Collection

Our extension does **not** collect any personal or sensitive user data. We do not gather, request, or transmit any personally identifiable information, authentication credentials, financial data, health information, communications, user activity, web browsing history, or any other sensitive data.

## Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal or sensitive user data, we do not use, process, or analyze any such data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not send any information to external servers.

## Data Storage

All extension settings and configurations are stored **locally in your browser** using browser-provided storage APIs (such as `chrome.storage.local`). No data is stored on external servers or cloud services. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing the extension's storage through your browser settings.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, transfer, or share any user data with third parties. Since no personal or sensitive user data is collected, there is no data to share or disclose.

## User Choices

You have full control over the extension and its data:

- **Disable the Extension**: You can disable or uninstall the extension at any time through your browser's settings.
- **Manage Permissions**: Adjust the permissions granted to the extension through your browser's extensions settings.
- **Clear Data**: Remove all locally stored extension data by uninstalling the extension or clearing its storage.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
